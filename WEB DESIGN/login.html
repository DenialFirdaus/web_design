About Us

At AustraFound, we bring the bold, rebellious spirit of opium-inspired and Y2K fashion to life. Our collections are designed for those who aren’t afraid to stand out, blending iconic styles from the late '90s and early 2000s with a modern twist. From oversized jackets and graphic tees to baggy jeans and statement accessories, our pieces capture the essence of two eras known for their creativity, individuality, and flair. We believe fashion is all about self-expression, and our mission is to empower you to showcase your unique style. Whether you're channeling a grunge vibe, embracing a futuristic look, or mixing both, AustraFound has everything you need to make a statement. Join us in celebrating fashion that’s timeless, bold, and unapologetically you.
